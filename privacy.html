1. Who we are

Loukik Sangha is a community and welfare organization in Bangladesh. We operate a website and an admin dashboard to manage members, donations, and transparency records.

2. Data we collect

  • Member details: member code, name, gender, join month, monthly due.
  • Contact (admin-only): phone number may be stored in a restricted admin collection.
  • Payments: amount, method (bKash/Nagad/Bank/Cash), month of payment, notes.
  • Admin account: admin email and authentication logs for security.
  • Technical data: device/browser data and IP-related logs may be processed by hosting or Firebase for security and fraud prevention.

3. How we use data

  • Manage members, dues, payments, and transparency records.
  • Prevent fraud and protect admin access.
  • Maintain financial reports (income/expense/fund).
  • Support requests, issue receipts or confirmations when applicable.

4. Legal basis & Bangladesh compliance

We handle personal data in line with applicable Bangladesh requirements, including data protection and cybersecurity obligations. Where required, we rely on consent, legitimate operational needs (member management and transparency records), and legal obligations.

For Bangladesh, keep your practices aligned with the national data protection framework (including the Personal Data Protection Ordinance, 2025 where applicable) and follow cybersecurity responsibilities for secure processing.

5. Sharing & third parties

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited data only:

  • Service providers: Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Hosting) to operate the platform.
  • Legal/safety: if required by law or to protect users and prevent misuse.

Third-party services may process data under their own terms and privacy policies (example: Google/Firebase).

6. Security

  • Admin-only access controls for sensitive fields (e.g., phone).
  • Role-based security rules in Firebase and restricted collections.
  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS) and security features provided by Firebase.

7. Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for community operations, transparency, and legal/accounting needs. Archived records may be kept for audit and reporting.

8. Your rights

You may request:

  • Access to your stored personal data (where identity can be verified).
  • Correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion of personal data (subject to lawful retention needs and record integrity).

9. Children

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Data deletion request (Play Store requirement)

To request deletion or correction of your personal data, email us with your member code and verification details:

Email: info@loukiksangha.org

We may ask for identity verification to prevent unauthorized deletion. Some records may be retained for legal, accounting, and transparency obligations.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy:

Location: Dhaka, Bangladesh

12. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page with an updated date.
